Welcome to LSIA

Los Santos International Airport (LSIA) is the primary airport serving the city of Los Santos. It is a hub for both domestic and international flights, providing travelers with a wide range of destinations.

Our airport is equipped with modern facilities and services to ensure a comfortable travel experience. From lounges to shopping and dining options, LSIA has everything you need before your flight.

History

LSIA has been serving the Los Santos community since its inception in the early 2000s. Over the years, it has grown to accommodate millions of passengers annually, becoming a vital part of the city's infrastructure.
